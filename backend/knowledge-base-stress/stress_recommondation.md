## Recommendation
Stress_Level: LOW  
Title: Eat Magnesium-Rich Foods

You can eat spinach, kale, and gotu kola.  
You can eat brown rice, oats, whole wheat, and fatty fish.  

These foods contain high magnesium which helps with:
- muscle relaxation
- nervous system balance
- better sleep
- reduced mild anxiety.


## Recommendation
Stress_Level: LOW  
Title: Eat Nuts and Seeds

Eat almonds, cashews, pumpkin seeds, and sunflower seeds.  
A small handful per day is enough.


## Recommendation
Stress_Level: LOW  
Title: Limit Certain Foods

Reduce coffee because too much caffeine increases heart rate and anxiety.  
Reduce high sugar snacks.  
Avoid high salt and unhealthy fats.  
Avoid heavy meals before sleep.

## Recommendation
Stress_Level: MODERATE  
Title: Breathing and Relaxation

Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 seconds.  
Hold your breath for 4 seconds.  
Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds.  
Repeat for 5–10 minutes.


## Recommendation
Stress_Level: MODERATE  
Title: Diaphragmatic Breathing (Belly Breathing)

Place one hand on your chest and one on your belly.  
Inhale deeply so your belly rises instead of your chest.  
Exhale slowly.  
Continue for 5 minutes.

## Recommendation

Follow this order:

Calves  
Thighs  
Stomach  
Hands  
Arms  
Shoulders  
Neck  
Face

Finish with slow breathing.

## Recommendation
Stress_Level: HIGH  
Title: Anapanasati Meditation

Choose a quiet place.

Sit cross-legged on the floor or on a chair.  
Keep your back straight but relaxed.  
Close your eyes gently.  
Place your hands on your lap.

Do not control your breathing.

Observe the natural breath as it comes in and goes out.

Focus attention on the sensation near the tip of the nose or upper lip.

Silently notice:

"In…"  
"Out…"


## Recommendation
Stress_Level: HIGH  
Title: Sleep Recovery Plan

Turn off screens 60 minutes before bed.  
Dim the lights.  
Avoid news and social media.

Take a warm shower or wash your face.  
Keep the room slightly cool.

Breathing exercise:

Inhale for 4 seconds.  
Hold for 7 seconds.  
Exhale for 8 seconds.

Repeat 6–8 times.

Additional tips:
- No caffeine after 2 PM  
- No heavy meals 2 hours before sleep  
- Avoid problem solving in bed  
- Aim for 6–7 hours of sleep.


## Recommendation
Stress_Level: HIGH  
Title: Talk to Someone You Trust

Choose someone who listens without judging.

Ask if you can talk for a few minutes.

Speak honestly about how you feel.  
Focus on your feelings instead of blaming others.

Allow yourself to be vulnerable.

After sharing, thank them and notice how your body feels calmer.
